The Story

James first laid eyes on Jessie the spring of 2009 - she was being loud after church service. He hid inside of a box in her room to ask to her prom (as her "present"), and their first date soon followed, consisting of Ethiopian food (they ate with their fingers) and a guitar serenade. After four years of long distance, snail mail, and many flight$ later, they were reunited in Austin, where they fell more in love with each other and ate lots of burgers together. Jessie and James now live and work five minutes from each other in Houston and unintentionally match outfits all the time. Eight years in (!!), they still debate about who trapped who and finally tying the knot so they can make lots of babies. They can't wait to share this special time with those that have loved, encouraged, and prayed for them throughout all these years.
